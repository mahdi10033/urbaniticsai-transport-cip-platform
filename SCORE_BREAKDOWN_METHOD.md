# Transparent Score Breakdown Method

This version adds a transparent score contribution engine.

## Formula

Weighted Contribution = Component Score × Weight / 100

Final Priority Score = Sum of all weighted contributions

## Example

If Safety Risk has:
- Component score = 90
- Weight = 20%

Then:

90 × 20 / 100 = 18 points

## Why this matters

This makes the scoring system transparent, explainable, auditable, and suitable for public-sector decision discussions.
