
# Configurable Strategic Alignment Framework

This framework allows agencies to define and customize their own transportation and infrastructure policy priorities.

Instead of hard-coding initiatives like Vision Zero or Complete Streets, agencies can define:
- strategic goals
- alignment weights
- required policy consistency
- custom scoring priorities

## Example use cases

- Safety-focused city using Vision Zero
- ADA-focused county transition planning
- Resilience-focused coastal municipality
- Growth-management focused MPO/TPO
- Transit-oriented development strategies

## Recommended workflow

1. Agency defines strategic priorities
2. Agency assigns weights
3. Projects are evaluated against those priorities
4. Strategic alignment scores feed into CIP prioritization

This creates a flexible policy-to-investment decision support framework.
